# Breathing Protocol

## Overview
This document outlines the 4-4-8 breathing protocol that helps in enhancing relaxation and mindfulness.

## The 4-4-8 Breathing Protocol
### Phase 1: Inhale
Inhale through your nose for 4 seconds.
### Phase 2: Hold
Hold your breath for 4 seconds.
### Phase 3: Exhale
Exhale through your mouth for 8 seconds.

## Essential Elements
- Find a quiet place to practice.
- Sit comfortably with your back straight.
- Close your eyes and focus on your breath.

## Optimization Tips
To enhance your fasting period and maximize autophagy activation, it is recommended to complete your last meal 2-3 hours before practicing the 4-4-8 breathing protocol. This approach not only supports uninterrupted deep sleep but also optimizes the benefits of the breathing practice.
